About_Me

Owiye Vincent is a soccer and Rugby player from Kenya. He was born to the third wife of his father, a railway attendant, and into a family of 16 children where he was the lastborn. He grew up in Siaya where he attended lower primary before moving to Nairobi to ewhere he graduated primary and secondary schools. He began playing soccer and rugby at a young age at Saili Oval Academy.

He was born in Ugenya, Siaya District (now Siaya County). Vincent ventured into playing sports immediately after completing primary school. It was a difficult decision because sports was not paying in Kenya and it is not considered as a career. However, Vincent's determination could not be stopped by the societal view of Sports and he pursued his talents and interests. After a long struggle, Vincent and his soccer friends joined kenya's division one league side Ligi Ndogo soccer academy after high school where he is playing as a goalkeeper.

Some of the most popular clubs Owiye Vincent has played are "Nyahurur Yanga (Laikipia County)", "Umoja FC (Baringo county)", "Egerton FC (Nakuru county)", "Nyahuruu All-stars (Laikipia county)" "Strathmore University Fc" and "Riara University FC". He played as first choice in most of the teams he played for and second choice to some teams.

In his career he has toured almost all counties just by the name of playing football. Only months before giving sports a rest in 2021, he won a schoolarship to join school where he is learning website software development at Moringa School .
