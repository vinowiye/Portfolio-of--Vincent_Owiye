About_Me

Owiye Vincent is a soccer and Rugby player from Kenya. I was born to the third wife of MY father, a railway attendant, and into a family of 16 children where I am the lastborn. I grew up in Siaya where I attended lower primary before moving to Nairobi to where I graduated with a primary and secondary school Certificate. I began playing soccer and rugby at a young age at Sadili Oval Academy.

I was born in Ugenya, Siaya District (now Siaya County). Vincent ventured into playing sports immediately after completing primary school. It was a difficult decision because sports was not paying in Kenya and it is not considered as a career. However, with determination I could not be stopped by the societal view of Sports and pursued my talents and interests. After a long struggle, my soccer friends and I joined kenya's division one league side Ligi Ndogo soccer academy after high school where I played as a goalkeeper.

Some of the most popular clubs I played for are "Nyahururu Yanga FC (Laikipia County)", "Umoja FC (Baringo county)", "Egerton FC (Nakuru county)", "Nyahuruu All-stars (Laikipia county)" "Strathmore University Fc" and "Riara University FC" among others. I played as first choice in most of the teams I played for and second choice to some teams.

In my football career I have toured almost all counties just by the name of playing football. Only months before giving sports a rest in 2021, I won a schoolarship to join school where am learning website and software development at Moringa School .
